\begin{frame}{Let's use some math!}
Let
\begin{align*}
X &\sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_1, \sigma^2_1), \\
Y &\sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_2, \sigma^2_2).
\end{align*}

Then $X + Y$ is also normally distributed and
$$ X + Y \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_1 + \mu_2, \sigma^2_1 + \sigma^2_2). $$
